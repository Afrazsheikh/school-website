Projects

  • "Generation" chooses reading"
    Since 2015, our school has been a regional branch of the Russian Reading Association (RACh). This is a non-profit public organization that brings together reading enthusiasts and specialists in various fields of knowledge: teachers and librarians, psychologists and sociologists of reading, journalists, publishers, etc.
